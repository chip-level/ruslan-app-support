## Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 22, 2026

These macOS apps do not collect, store, sell, share, or track personal data.

The apps do not require an account, do not use advertising, do not include in-app purchases, and do not use analytics or third-party tracking SDKs.

Some app functions may work with data that you enter or process locally on your Mac. This data stays on your device and is not sent to the developer by the app.
